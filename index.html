About me

Rooted in community,
reaching outward.

Piyush Rana is a 15-year-old student at Nanakmatta Public School, Uttarakhand, belonging to the indigenous Tharu tribe of U.S. Nagar. He believes meaningful learning comes from people and the environment around him.

His work spans visual storytelling, environmental action, cultural preservation, and social advocacy — always grounded in the idea of being of service to his community rather than chasing recognition alone.

From documenting biodiversity in the field to co-managing a creative Instagram platform, Piyush weaves curiosity and commitment into everything he does.
